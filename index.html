The Story of the Year of Community

President of the UAE, His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan has announced 2025 as the Year of Community under the tagline “Hand in Hand”– a nationwide initiative that will run from January until December 2025.

Rooted in the UAE’s values of resilience, ambition, and openness, the Year of Community is dedicated to fostering a united and empowered society. It aims to inspire behaviours that strengthen family and social ties, encourage shared responsibility, and unlock potential for sustainable growth.

Unite

Reinforcing community bonds within families and society at large – preserving cultural heritage, nurturing intergenerational relationships, and creating inclusive spaces that encourage collaboration, a sense of belonging, and shared experiences.

Contribute

Promoting a culture of shared responsibility, encouraging all who call the UAE home to actively engage in society through service, volunteering, and meaningful initiatives – fostering mutual care and driving collective progress.

Grow

Unlocking the potential of individuals, families, and organisations – building skills, developing talent, and embracing innovation in areas such as entrepreneurship and emerging industries like AI and other national priority sectors, to enable inclusive growth, skill sharing, and create a lasting positive impact.
